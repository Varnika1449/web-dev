Pizza

Pizza is an Italian dish of baked bread dough topped with sauce and other ingredients. It's one of the world's most popular foods. History The word "pizza" may come from an Old Italian word meaning "a point". The term "pizza" was first recorded in 997 AD in a Latin manuscript. Raffaele Esposito is often credited with inventing modern pizza in Naples in the 19th century. Ingredients Pizza is typically made with tomato-based sauce, but butter-based sauces are also used. Common toppings include mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, sausages, vegetables, tomatoes, spices, herbs, and basil.
